About Me

Ryan found himself in Los Angeles after growing up in Houston, TX. On any given day, you might find him wandering the streets of LA, contemplating his next meal, and wondering why more people haven't realized that after leaving home, Smokey from Friday grew up to be Detective James Carter from Rush Hour.

With a background in Design and Communications, Ryan has his sights set on improving his skills and gaining more knowledge on the way to finding a worthwhile career.
